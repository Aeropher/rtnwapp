Why RightNow Works

Built by a solo developer who understands the struggle of unrealistic planning, RightNow succeeds where other apps fail:

Time is Finite

Every day has limited productive hours. RightNow enforces this reality with visual capacity bars and instant feedback when you exceed your limits.

Offline-First

Lightning fast UI with no waiting for network calls. Works perfectly without internet and syncs when connected.

Smart Analytics

Learn from your patterns with productivity insights, estimation accuracy tracking, and personalized recommendations.

Focus Mode

Full-screen active task view with subtask support. Distraction-free interface when you're ready to work.
